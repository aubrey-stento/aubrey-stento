About

Prior to Boise CodeWorks, I was working management in the food industry. I knew that I did not want to remain in the industry forever, so I started applying for various entry level customer service jobs. Following several failed attempts to secure a position, I was feeling frustrated that I was not able to utilize my education, or feel challenged in current jobs. My parents had been encouraging me to look into coding and web development based on my love for math and strategy. That is when I discovered Boise Codeworks. Since beginning the course, I have been blown away with my passion and excitement for learning new languages, and how many different ways there are to accomplish any given task or functionality. I could not be more excited for what a career in software development holds for me and my future.
